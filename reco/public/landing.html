Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Take a look here.

How does Reco work?
Reco analyzes your existing customer reviews using AI and allows shoppers to ask natural language questions about your products. It provides instant, relevant answers based on real customer feedback.
How do I install Reco on my store?
Simply install Reco from the Shopify App Store, then enable the app embed in your theme editor. No coding required - setup takes about 2 minutes.
Where do you get review data from?
Reco integrates with popular review platforms like Okendo, Judge.me, Stamped, and Yotpo. We pull your existing reviews and analyze them to power the AI assistant.
How accurate are the AI responses?
Reco grounds all responses in your actual customer reviews - no hallucinations. Every answer is based on real feedback from people who've purchased your products.
Is my customer data safe?
Absolutely. We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never share your customer information with third parties.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel anytime directly from your Shopify admin. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
