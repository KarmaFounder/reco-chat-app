Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install Reco on my store?

Simply visit the Shopify App Store and search for "Reco" or click the "Install App" button on our website. Follow the installation prompts, and you'll be set up in under 2 minutes.

Which review platforms does Reco support?

Reco currently integrates with Okendo, Judge.me, and Shopify's native reviews. We're continuously adding more integrations based on merchant feedback.

How does Reco's AI work?

Reco analyzes your product reviews and uses advanced AI to understand the context and sentiment. When customers ask questions, our AI provides answers grounded entirely in real customer feedback—no hallucinations or made-up information.

Can I customize the widget appearance?

Yes! Reco's widget is fully customizable to match your brand. You can adjust colors, position, and messaging through the app settings.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a free trial so you can test Reco on your store before committing. No credit card required to start.

How do I uninstall Reco?

You can uninstall Reco at any time through your Shopify admin panel under Apps. Your data will be deleted within 30 days of uninstallation.
